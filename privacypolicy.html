Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Postra ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

When you create a Postra account, we collect your name, email address, niche, and creator preferences you provide during onboarding.

Instagram integration data

When you connect your Instagram account, we access data permitted by the Instagram API — including your profile, follower count, and post performance data. We do not store your Instagram password. We use only the permissions you explicitly grant.

Usage data

We collect information on how you use Postra — which features you use, how often you post, and content creation patterns. This helps us improve the product and personalise your experience.

Payment information

Payments are processed by a third-party provider. We do not store your credit card or banking details on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Postra service
  • To generate AI-powered content suggestions based on your niche and tone
  • To send relevant product updates and creator tips (you can opt out anytime)
  • To analyse usage patterns and improve features
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions

3. Instagram Integration

Postra integrates with Instagram via the official Meta API. We only access data you explicitly authorise. You can revoke access at any time from your Instagram settings. We do not share your Instagram data with third parties.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and understand how our site is used. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with service providers (hosting, analytics, payment) who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements. We may disclose data if required by law.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure servers, and access controls. While no system is 100% secure, we take your data protection seriously.

7. Your Rights

  • Access and export your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
