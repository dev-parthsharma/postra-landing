Step 4 — Shooting Guide Pro

Walk into filming knowing exactly what to shoot.

Most creators know what to say — they just don't know how to film it. Postra breaks your script into scenes and tells you exactly: camera angle, movement, framing, and background for each shot.

No more filming for 2 hours and ending up with unusable footage. Every shot is intentional.

📸 Shooting guide
Scene 1: Face-to-camera, window light, slight low angle. Speak directly into the lens.
Scene 2: B-roll of gym. Quick 0.5s cuts. Slow pan. No shaky cam.
Scene 3: CTA closeup — zoom in slightly, look into camera, speak slowly.
📲 All scenes linked directly to your script

Step 5 — Editing Guide Pro

Edit your reel with direction, not guesswork.

After filming, Postra suggests transitions, pacing, music, and text overlay placement — matched to your content type and vibe. Beginner-friendly, no editing experience needed.

Know exactly what to cut, when to cut, and what music sets the right tone.

✂️ Editing guide
Pacing: Fast cuts (0.3–0.5s) on Points 1–3. Slow down on the CTA.
Transitions: Jump cuts. No wipes or dissolves — keep it clean.
Text overlays: Show each mistake as text on screen, timed to your words.
✨ Music: lo-fi trap beat, 95 BPM — matches your pacing

Step 6–7 — Caption, Hashtags & Publishing

This Cookie Policy explains how Postra uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you use our platform.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They allow websites to remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

2. Cookies We Use

Essential cookies

These are required for Postra to function. They keep you logged in and maintain your session. Without these, the platform cannot work. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies

We use analytics tools to understand how users navigate Postra. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

Preference cookies

These remember your settings — like your preferred language, billing region, and UI preferences.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our payment processor may set cookies to facilitate secure transactions. These are governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent some Postra features from working correctly.

5. Cookie Duration

  • Session cookies: deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: remain until you delete them or they expire (typically 30–365 days)
