Features

Every feature has one job.

Remove friction. From the moment you have an idea to the moment you hit post — Postra keeps you moving forward.

Idea Generation

Never stare at a blank screen again.

Postra analyses your niche, your past content, and current trends to generate 3 relevant ideas on demand. Each idea is specific — not generic. You'll see ideas you'd actually want to make.

Don't like them? Regenerate. Love one? Select it and move to hooks instantly. Want to save the others for later? Saved to your idea bank.

Hook + Script System

Your first 3 seconds determine everything.

Postra generates 3 distinct hook variations for every idea — question-style, bold statement, or story-opener. Each one is designed around proven scroll-stopping formats.

Once you choose a hook, Postra writes the full script — matched to your tone (professional, casual, energetic) and your niche. You can read it, tweak it, or use it as-is.

3 hook styles Tone-matched scripts Scene-by-scene breakdown
🪝 Choose your hook
Hook A: "Most people never see results because they skip this one step…"
Hook B: "I started with zero knowledge. Here's what actually worked for me."
Hook C: "Stop doing this if you want to see real progress."

Shoot & Edit Guidance

A creative director in your pocket.

Most creators know what to say but not how to film it. Postra breaks your script into scenes and tells you exactly how to shoot each one — camera angle, movement, background suggestions.

After filming, Postra suggests editing effects, transitions, pacing, and music — based on your content type and vibe. No editing experience required.

🎬 Scene guidance
Scene 1: Face-to-camera, natural lighting, slight low angle for confidence. Say the hook directly.
Scene 2: B-roll of gym. Quick cuts, 0.5s each. Use trending audio underneath.
Scene 3: CTA closeup — look into camera, speak slowly. End with a smirk.
✨ Suggested music: lo-fi trap beat, 95 BPM

Captions, Hashtags & Scheduling

Post-ready in under a minute.

After you upload your content, Postra generates 3 caption variations — short, medium, and long-form. Each is written in your brand voice. Pick one, edit it, or regenerate.

Add up to 5 targeted hashtags (chosen from a generated set of 15+), pick your cover, add music, and either post immediately or schedule for the optimal time AI suggests.

✍️ Caption options
Short: "This one habit changed everything. 🔥 #fitness #growthmindset"
Medium: "Took me 2 years to figure this out — and I'm sharing it so you don't waste time. Drop a 🙋 if this helped. #fitnesshacks #reels"
⏰ Best time to post: Tuesday 6:30 PM IST

Saved Workflows

Your best process — on repeat.

After any content session, Postra asks: "Save this workflow?" If you say yes, all your settings — content type, tone, hook style, caption format — are saved as a one-click template.

Next time you create, just select your saved workflow. No AI chat needed. Just step-by-step guided selections. 10x faster content creation.

This Cookie Policy explains how Postra uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you use our platform.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They allow websites to remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

2. Cookies We Use

Essential cookies

These are required for Postra to function. They keep you logged in and maintain your session. Without these, the platform cannot work. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies

We use analytics tools (such as a self-hosted analytics solution) to understand how users navigate Postra. This data is aggregated and anonymous — we cannot identify individual users from it.

Preference cookies

These remember your settings — like your preferred language, billing region, and UI preferences.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our payment processor may set cookies to facilitate secure transactions. These are governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent some Postra features from working correctly.
